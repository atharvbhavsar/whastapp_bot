GPC Barmer - College Information Document

PRINCIPAL AND ADMINISTRATION
The Principal of Government Polytechnic College Barmer is Dr. Rajesh Kumar. His office is located in the Administration Block. For official queries, you can reach the principal at 0298-2220634 or send emails to principal@gpcbarmer.edu.in. Office timings are Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

ENGINEERING DEPARTMENTS OVERVIEW
The college houses five main engineering departments:

Civil Engineering Department - Led by Prof. Ramesh Patel, focuses on structural design and infrastructure projects. Contact through extension 2201.

Mechanical Engineering Department - Headed by Prof. Suresh Kumar, specializes in machinery and thermal systems. Reach at ext. 2202.

Electrical Engineering Department - Under Prof. Vikram Singh's leadership, covers power systems and electrical machines. Extension 2203.

Electronics and Communication Department - Led by Prof. Priya Sharma, focuses on digital and analog circuits. Contact ext. 2204.

Computer Science and Engineering Department - Managed by Prof. Anil Verma, specializes in software and programming. Ext. 2205.

FACILITIES AVAILABLE ON CAMPUS
The college provides excellent infrastructure:
- A comprehensive library with over 50,000 books and reference materials
- Five computer labs equipped with 200+ latest computers
- Modern workshops with advanced machinery
- An auditorium that can accommodate up to 500 people
- Sports facilities including basketball courts, badminton courts, and cricket grounds
- Accommodation: Boys hostel with 200 seats, Girls hostel with 100 seats
- A well-stocked cafeteria serving meals at affordable prices
- Medical center providing basic healthcare

The college offers various financial assistance:
- Merit-based aid: Top 10% get 50% discount
- Government scholarships: SC/ST category eligible
- Financial need awards: For families earning under Rs. 2.5 lakhs annually
- Athletic scholarships: For sports excellence
