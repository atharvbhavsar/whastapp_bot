COLLEGE OVERVIEW

ADMISSION PROCESS
Eligibility: Pass 10+2 or equivalent examination
Merit-based selection through state counseling
Reservation: SC/ST/OBC as per government norms
Merit List: Published on college website and displayed on notice board
