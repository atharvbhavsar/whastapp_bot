About Demo College
------------------
Demo College is a premier educational institution established in 2020. We offer 
world-class education in Computer Science, Engineering, and Business Administration.

Campus Location: Bangalore, Karnataka
Established: 2020
Accreditation: NAAC A+ Grade
Student Strength: 5,000+ students

Admission Requirements
----------------------
To apply for admission to Demo College, students must meet the following criteria:

Undergraduate Programs:
- Minimum 75% marks in 12th grade (PCM for Engineering, Any stream for BBA)
- Valid entrance exam score (JEE Main for Engineering, CUET for BBA)
- Age limit: 17-22 years at the time of admission

Postgraduate Programs:
- Bachelor's degree with minimum 60% marks
- Valid GATE/CAT score (depending on program)
- Work experience (optional but preferred for MBA)

Documents Required:
1. 10th standard marksheet and certificate
2. 12th standard marksheet and certificate
3. Entrance exam scorecard
4. Transfer certificate from previous institution
5. Character certificate
6. Caste certificate (if applicable for reservation)
7. Income certificate (for fee concessions)
8. Aadhar card and passport size photographs
